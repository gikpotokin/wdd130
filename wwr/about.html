History

Founded in 2005, our company has guided thousands of adventurers down the wildest rivers across the country. With expert guides and a dedication to safety, we continue to create memories that last a lifetime.

Our passion for adventure and respect for nature fuel our mission to provide thrilling experiences while protecting the rivers we love. We believe rafting is more than a sport—it’s a journey into the wild.
